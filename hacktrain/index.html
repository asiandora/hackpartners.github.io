Initiatives ran under HackTrain


HackTrain Hackathons

48-hour weekend technology event occurring annually in November. 120++ developers, designers and entrepreneurs take part to build solutions and new product and innovations to solve problems in the rail industry. Three different trains take the participants across the UK and mainland Europe to various locations as they test and produce their products.

Join us for HackTrain 4.0!

HackTrain Accelerator

To ignite the Rail industry, we ran a 3-month programme housing 10 established startups from other industries to implement their technologies into rail. The programme was supported by five major owning groups: GWR, Arriva, National Express, Go-Ahead and Stagecoach; and top level service partners including EY, Osborne Clarke, BAE Systems and the Novacroft Group.

HackTrain Conference

The HackTrain Conference marked the end of the HackTrain accelerator and showcased the results of all 10 startups that were part of the 3-month accelerator programme. During the full day event, we also focused on communicating and enabling innovation in the rail industry by introducing speakers of both the tech and rail industry to share their knowledge.

B.A.R.R.I.E.R.S. Report

An acronym of Bringing Actionable Recommendations to Revitalise Innovation and Entrepreneurship in the Rail Sector, the B.A.R.R.I.E.R.S. Report highlights the barriers to innovation in the rail industry and provides actionable recommendations to combat these barriers and improve innovation.
